Any resemblance to real persons, living or dead, or actual events is purely coincidental—unless it's not, in which case we still probably made it up.

We do our best to label clearly what is parody, but readers are expected to bring their critical thinking (and maybe a sense of humor). The views expressed in articles, comics, or images belong to the authors and do not necessarily reflect those of The Pressive as a whole.

If you're unsure whether something is real or not, assume it's a joke—and then double-check elsewhere just to be safe. You can also ask us directly at hello@your-domain.com
